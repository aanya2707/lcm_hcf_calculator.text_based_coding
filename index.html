HCF

Use the free online HCF calculator to find the highest number which divides all the numbers in the given list. You can enter two or more numbers and click calculate, the highest common factor calculator will update you the result. HCF is the largest positive number that divides the given numbers, without any reminder. For example: highest common factor of 60 and 75 = 15 because 15 is the highest number which divides both 60 and 75 exactly. Similarly, HCF of 24 and 36 is 12, because the common factors of 24 and 36 = 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8 and 12. Hence, the Highest Common Factor (H.C.F) of 24 and 36 is 12 and for 24, 36 and 56 it is 4.

Example
Find HCF of 12, 20 and 18

Factors of 12 = 2×2×3
Factors of 18 = 2×3×3
Factors of 20 = 2×2×5
The common factors of 12, 18 and 20 are : 2
Hence, 2 is the Highest Common Factor of 18, 20 and 12.
